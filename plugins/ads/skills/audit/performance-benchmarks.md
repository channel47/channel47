# Google Ads Performance Benchmarks

Industry average performance metrics for evaluating account health.

---

## How to Use These Benchmarks

These are **directional guides**, not absolute targets:
- Your actual performance depends on many factors
- Use for relative comparison, not hard rules
- Benchmarks vary by source and methodology
- Update annually as industry shifts

| Score | Rating | Expected Performance |
|-------|--------|---------------------|
| 8-10 | Excellent | Above average CTR, highly relevant |
| 6-7 | Good | Meeting expectations |
| 4-5 | Below Average | Room for improvement |

## Impression Share Benchmarks

### Search Impression Share

| Level | Impression Share | Interpretation |
|-------|-----------------|----------------|
| Dominant | 80%+ | Strong market position |
| Competitive | 50-80% | Healthy competition |
| Developing | 30-50% | Room to grow |
| Limited | <30% | Budget or rank constrained |

### Lost Impression Share

| Type | Concern Level | Action |
|------|---------------|--------|
| Budget Lost IS > 30% | High | Increase budget or reduce scope |
| Budget Lost IS 10-30% | Medium | Monitor, consider budget increase |
| Rank Lost IS > 40% | High | Improve QS, increase bids |
| Rank Lost IS 20-40% | Medium | Optimize ads and landing pages |

## Account-Level Health Indicators

### Healthy Account Signs

- Conversion rate improving over time
- Cost per conversion stable or decreasing
- Quality Score average 6+
- No campaigns with 0 conversions and significant spend
- Search terms report reviewed regularly
- Negative keywords actively managed
- Ad copy testing in progress

### Warning Signs

- Declining conversion rate
- Rising CPA without explanation
- Quality Score average below 5
- Large budget lost impression share
- Many keywords with 0 impressions
- Stale ad copy (no tests in 90+ days)
- No audience signals configured

### Critical Issues

- Zero conversions account-wide
- Broken conversion tracking
- All campaigns limited by budget
- Search terms report shows >50% irrelevant queries
- Landing pages returning errors

## Using Benchmarks in Audits

### Comparison Framework

| Performance vs Benchmark | Rating | Action |
|-------------------------|--------|--------|
| 20%+ above | Excellent | Scale what's working |
| Within 20% | Good | Optimize incrementally |
| 20-50% below | Needs Work | Identify issues, test improvements |
| 50%+ below | Critical | Major restructure needed |

### Context Matters

Always consider:
- Account maturity (new accounts perform differently)
- Seasonality
- Competitive landscape
- Product/service type
- Price point
- Sales cycle length
