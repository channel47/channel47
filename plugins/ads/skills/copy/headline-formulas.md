# Headline Formulas

Fill-in-the-blank patterns, psychological triggers, and dynamic keyword insertion for high-converting ad headlines.

---

## Fill-in-the-Blank Formulas

Proven structures with 30-character versions for ads.

### Number Headlines

Numbers catch the eye and set expectations.

| Formula | Example | Chars |
|---------|---------|-------|
| `[#] Ways to [Benefit]` | 7 Ways to Save on Insurance | 25 |
| `[#] [Things] Every [Person] Needs` | 5 Tools Every Chef Needs | 23 |
| `[#]-Step [Process] for [Result]` | 3-Step System for Clear Skin | 27 |
| `Get [#]% [Better/More/Off]` | Get 40% More Battery Life | 24 |
| `[#] [People] Can't Be Wrong` | 50,000 Customers Can't Be Wrong | 30 |
| `In Just [#] [Time]` | Results in Just 7 Days | 21 |
| `[#]x [Faster/Better/Stronger]` | 3x Faster Than Competitors | 25 |
| `Save [#]+ Hours [Per Time]` | Save 10+ Hours Per Week | 21 |

**Character-saving tips:**
- Use numerals: "7" not "seven"
- Use "+" for "or more": "50+" vs "50 or more"
- Use "k" for thousands: "10k" vs "10,000"

### Question Headlines

Questions create curiosity gaps that pull readers in.

| Formula | Example | Chars |
|---------|---------|-------|
| `Still [Struggling With Problem]?` | Still Fighting Pet Odors? | 23 |
| `Why [Do/Are] [People] [Action]?` | Why Do Pros Choose Us? | 21 |
| `What If [Desired State]?` | What If Cleaning Was Easy? | 24 |
| `Tired of [Pain Point]?` | Tired of Slow Internet? | 22 |
| `Ready for [Positive Outcome]?` | Ready for Clear Skin? | 20 |
| `What's Your [Problem] Costing You?` | What's Bad Sleep Costing? | 24 |
| `Is [Current Solution] Working?` | Is Your Diet Working? | 20 |
| `Who Else Wants [Benefit]?` | Who Else Wants More Time? | 24 |

**When to use questions:**
- Problem-aware audiences (PAS framework)
- When the problem is emotionally charged
- When you want engagement over information

**When to avoid:**
- Most-aware audiences (they know the answer)
- When the answer might be "no"

## Psychological Triggers

Principles that drive action, applied to 30-character headlines.

### 1. Loss Aversion

People feel losses 2x more than gains.

| Trigger | Gain Frame (Weaker) | Loss Frame (Stronger) |
|---------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| Money | Save $50 Today | Stop Wasting $50/Month |
| Time | Get 2 Hours Back | Losing 2 Hours Daily? |
| Results | Get Better Sleep | Missing Quality Sleep? |
| Opportunity | Get 20% Off | Don't Miss 20% Off |

**Patterns:**
- "Stop [wasting/losing/missing]..."
- "Don't let [problem] cost you..."
- "You're [losing/missing] [benefit]"
- "[Problem] is costing you [amount]"

---

### 2. Social Proof

What others do signals what we should do.

| Type | Headline Pattern | Example |
|------|-----------------|---------|
| Numbers | `[#]+ [people] chose [product]` | 150,000+ Chose Our Plan |
| Reviews | `[Rating] Stars from [#] Reviews` | 4.8 Stars, 5,000 Reviews |
| Authority | `[Expert] Recommended` | Dentist Recommended |
| Peer | `Join Fellow [Identity]` | Join Fellow Entrepreneurs |
| Trending | `[Year]'s Most Popular [Category]` | 2024's Top Mattress |

**Most persuasive order:**
1. Specific numbers (127,432 > "thousands")
2. Relevant peer group (your tribe)
3. Authority endorsement
4. Trending/popularity

---

### 3. Scarcity

Limited availability increases perceived value.

| Type | Pattern | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| Quantity | `Only [#] Left` | Only 8 Left in Stock |
| Time | `[Offer] Until [Deadline]` | Free Shipping Until Friday |
| Access | `Limited to [#] [People]` | Limited to 500 Members |
| Edition | `[Season/Year] Limited Edition` | 2024 Limited Edition |
| Batch | `Small Batch [Product]` | Small Batch Release |

**Ethical scarcity rules:**
- Must be true
- Vague scarcity ("limited time") is less effective than specific ("ends Friday")
- Explain why it's limited if possible

---

### 4. Authority

Expertise and credentials build trust.

| Authority Type | Pattern | Example |
|---------------|---------|---------|
| Credential | `[Certification]-[Approved/Cleared]` | FDA-Cleared Device |
| Expert | `[Expert Type] Developed` | Dermatologist Developed |
| Institution | `As Used by [Institution]` | As Used by Harvard |
| Award | `[Award] Winner` | Editor's Choice Winner |
| Years | `[#] Years of Experience` | 25 Years of Excellence |

---

### 5. Reciprocity

Give value first to create obligation.

| Pattern | Example | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| `Free [Valuable Thing]` | Free Shipping on All Orders | Most common |
| `Get [Gift] With Purchase` | Free Case With Phone | Bonus gift |
| `[#]-Day Free Trial` | 30-Day Free Trial | Try before buy |
| `No [Fee/Commitment]` | No Setup Fees Ever | Remove friction |
| `[Free Resource] Inside` | Free Recipe Book Inside | Content offer |

---

### 6. Commitment/Consistency

Small yeses lead to big yeses.

| Pattern | Purpose | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| `Take the First Step` | Low commitment | Start Your Free Trial |
| `See If [Product] Is Right` | Self-selection | See If You Qualify |
| `Just [Simple Action]` | Minimize effort | Just Add Water |
| `[Quick Action] Now` | Immediate small ask | Check Your Rate Now |

---

### 7. Curiosity Gap

Open a loop that demands closure.

| Pattern | Example | Why It Works |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| `The [Secret/Truth] About [Topic]` | The Truth About Protein | Implies hidden knowledge |
| `What [Experts] Know About [X]` | What Doctors Know | Insider information |
| `The [Adjective] Mistake [People] Make` | The Costly DIY Mistake | Fear of error |
| `Why [Surprising Fact]` | Why Diets Fail You | Counterintuitive |
| `This [Thing] Changes Everything` | This Tool Changes Cooking | Bold promise |

**Caution:** Must deliver on the curiosity. Bait-and-switch kills trust.

## Dynamic Keyword Insertion (Search RSA)

Insert search terms directly into headlines for relevance.

### Syntax

```
{KeyWord:Default Text}
```

- `KeyWord` = inserts the triggering keyword with title case
- `Default Text` = shows if keyword is too long for character limit

### Capitalization Options

| Syntax | Keyword "running shoes" Becomes |
|--------|--------------------------------|
| `{keyword:default}` | running shoes |
| `{Keyword:default}` | Running shoes |
| `{KeyWord:default}` | Running Shoes |
| `{KEYWORD:default}` | RUNNING SHOES |

### Patterns for DKI

| Pattern | Example | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| `Shop {KeyWord:Products}` | Shop Running Shoes | Generic fallback |
| `Best {KeyWord:Deals}` | Best Running Shoes | Category relevance |
| `{KeyWord:Items} on Sale` | Running Shoes on Sale | Offer + keyword |
| `Find Your {KeyWord:Perfect Fit}` | Find Your Running Shoes | Search intent |
| `Top-Rated {KeyWord:Products}` | Top-Rated Running Shoes | Social proof |

### When to Use DKI

**Good uses:**
- High-volume, similar keywords in one ad group
- Generic product categories
- Location insertion: `{KeyWord:Your Area}`

**Avoid when:**
- Keywords are too varied (grammatically awkward)
- Brand campaigns (looks weird with your own brand)
- Keywords have typos (they'll insert typos)
- Emotional/problem keywords ("fix my broken marriage" as headline = bad)

### Character Limit Math

The default must fit within 30 characters including surrounding text.

| Headline Structure | Available for Default |
|-------------------|----------------------|
| `Buy {KeyWord:X}` | 26 chars for X |
| `Best {KeyWord:X} Online` | 19 chars for X |
| `{KeyWord:X} - Free Shipping` | 14 chars for X |

### DKI Checklist

- [ ] Default text makes sense alone
- [ ] All keywords in ad group are grammatically compatible
- [ ] No keywords with typos
- [ ] Tested at max keyword length
- [ ] Not using for brand terms

## Headline Angle Coverage

Every ad group needs headline diversity. Use this matrix to ensure coverage.

### Required Angles (one each minimum)

| Angle | Purpose | Formula Examples |
|-------|---------|------------------|
| **Problem** | Show understanding | "Still [struggling with X]?", "[Problem]? We Get It" |
| **Benefit** | Paint the outcome | "[Outcome] in [Time]", "Finally, [Desired State]" |
| **Proof** | Build credibility | "[#] [Star] Reviews", "Trusted by [#]+" |
| **Value** | Address price concern | "[Price]", "Save [Amount]", "[%] Off" |
| **Objection** | Preempt resistance | "No [Concern]", "[Guarantee]", "Risk-Free" |

### Recommended Angles (add 2-3)

| Angle | Purpose | Formula Examples |
|-------|---------|------------------|
| **Urgency** | Drive immediate action | "[Offer] Ends [Time]", "Limited [X]" |
| **Identity** | Appeal to tribe | "For [Identity]", "The [Identity] Choice" |
| **Mechanism** | Explain how | "[Feature] for [Benefit]", "[Method] That Works" |
| **Brand** | Build recognition | "[Brand Name] [Tagline]", "Official [Brand]" |

### Angle Combinations

Mix angles for variety without redundancy:

```
Headline 1: [Problem angle]
Headline 2: [Benefit angle - primary]
Headline 3: [Benefit angle - secondary]
Headline 4: [Proof angle]
Headline 5: [Value angle]
Headline 6: [Objection angle]
Headline 7: [Benefit + Proof combined]
Headline 8: [Urgency angle]
Headline 9: [Identity angle]
Headline 10: [Brand angle]
```
