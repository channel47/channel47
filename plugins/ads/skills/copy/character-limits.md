## Performance Max (PMax)

### Short Headlines

| Specification | Requirement |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Character limit** | 30 |
| **Minimum required** | 3 |
| **Maximum allowed** | 15 |
| **Recommended** | 11-15 for maximum combinations |

**Best Practices:**
- Use ALL 15 slots when possible
- Vary lengths (some 15 chars, some 30)
- Include at least 1 with brand name
- Mix benefit, feature, and proof headlines

### Long Headlines

| Specification | Requirement |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Character limit** | 90 |
| **Minimum required** | 1 |
| **Maximum allowed** | 5 |
| **Recommended** | 5 |

**Best Practices:**
- Expand on short headlines with more detail
- Used prominently in YouTube and Discovery
- Include benefit + proof combination
- Can include CTA within limit

### Descriptions

| Specification | Requirement |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Character limit** | 90 |
| **Minimum required** | 2 |
| **Maximum allowed** | 5 |
| **Recommended** | 4-5 |

**Best Practices:**
- First description is most important
- Include key differentiators
- End with clear CTA
- Add social proof where possible

### Business Name

| Specification | Requirement |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Character limit** | 25 |
| **Required** | Yes |

## Search RSA (Responsive Search Ads)

### Headlines

| Specification | Requirement |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Character limit** | 30 |
| **Minimum required** | 3 |
| **Maximum allowed** | 15 |
| **Recommended** | 10-15 |

**Pinning Options:**
- Pin 1: Always shows in position 1
- Pin 2: Always shows in position 2
- Pin 3: Always shows in position 3
- Unpinned: Rotates across all positions

**Best Practices:**
- Provide 10+ headlines minimum
- Pin sparingly (reduces combinations)
- Include keyword in 2-3 headlines
- Include brand in 1-2 headlines

### Descriptions

| Specification | Requirement |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Character limit** | 90 |
| **Minimum required** | 2 |
| **Maximum allowed** | 4 |
| **Recommended** | 4 |

**Best Practices:**
- Description 1 shows most often
- Can pin descriptions to positions
- Include CTA in at least one
- Vary message angles

### Display URL Paths

| Specification | Requirement |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Character limit** | 15 per path |
| **Paths available** | 2 |

**Example:** `example.com/Path1/Path2`

## Validation Rules

### Prohibited Patterns

| Rule | Bad | Good |
|------|-----|------|
| **Trailing punctuation on headlines** | `Free Shipping.` | `Free Shipping` |
| **Excessive exclamation** | `Buy Now!!!` | `Buy Now` |
| **Excessive question marks** | `Why Wait???` | `Why Wait?` |
| **ALL CAPS words** | `BEST DEAL EVER` | `Best Deal Ever` |
| **Phone numbers in text** | `Call 555-1234` | Use call extensions |
| **Trademark symbols** | `NikeÂ®` | `Nike` |
| **Emoji characters** | `Save ðŸ’° Today` | `Save Today` |

### Allowed Exceptions

- ALL CAPS acronyms: `FDA`, `USDA`, `HEPA`
- ALL CAPS common abbreviations: `TV`, `AC`, `USA`
- Single exclamation: `Shop Now!`
- Single question mark: `Why Wait?`

## Character Counting Notes

### What Counts
- All letters (a-z, A-Z)
- All numbers (0-9)
- Spaces
- Punctuation marks
- Special characters (&, %, $, etc.)

### Common Character Traps

| Text | Appears | Actual Chars |
|------|---------|--------------|
| `$99` | 3 chars | 3 chars |
| `50% Off` | 7 chars | 7 chars |
| `5-Star` | 6 chars | 6 chars |
| `#1 Rated` | 8 chars | 8 chars |
| `30-Day` | 6 chars | 6 chars |

### Space-Saving Tips

| Long Form | Short Form | Saves |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| `and` | `&` | 2 chars |
| `percent` | `%` | 6 chars |
| `number one` | `#1` | 8 chars |
| `30 days` | `30-Day` | 2 chars |
| `Free shipping` | `Free Ship` | 4 chars |

## Ad Strength Scoring

Google rates your ad based on asset quantity and quality:

| Ad Strength | Meaning | Action |
|-------------|---------|--------|
| **Excellent** | Maximum combinations, diverse messaging | Maintain |
| **Good** | Sufficient assets, some optimization possible | Consider adding variety |
| **Average** | Missing assets or low diversity | Add more headlines/descriptions |
| **Poor** | Below minimum or repetitive | Immediate attention needed |

### Factors Affecting Strength

1. **Quantity**: More assets = more combinations
2. **Diversity**: Different angles and messages
3. **Keyword relevance**: Headlines matching ad group keywords
4. **Uniqueness**: No duplicate or similar headlines
